Objective of Task.Done

This platform enables teams to create project rooms, facilitating task assignment and leveraging OpenAI for task summarization and step-by-step guidance.

What does Task.Done do?

This platform is like a helper for teams doing projects together. It lets them make special rooms for their projects where they can give out tasks and keep things organized. The cool part is it works with OpenAI, which helps by giving short summaries of tasks and telling the team what to do step by step. This makes it easier for everyone to work together and finish their tasks on time.
